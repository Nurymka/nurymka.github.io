Revamping the content deployment pipeline (contentful-export)

Robinhood has various documents, agreements and warnings across the app powered by Contentful CMS. Any time a change was made in Contentful, it required two engineers to take a snapshot of the current version of Contentful, generate proper formatting through an Xcode project, upload the latest version to the monorepo, and upload it to Amazon S3.

I designed & developed a package of node scripts that simplifies the deployment pipeline, and laid the foundation for further automation of the deployment process.

Kanji Ninja

I designed & developed a fun game to learn both drawing and reading of 1,000 Japanese characters. You can learn kanji on the go through engaging mini-sessions where you learn 3 characters at a time, both stroke order and meaning. Built-in optical character recognition (OCR) engine can check whether you drew the characters correctly. The adaptive engine tracks your progress and prompts you to review kanji at just the right time. Built @ nFactorial Incubator 2015. Featured on Product Hunt (with 270+ upvotes).
